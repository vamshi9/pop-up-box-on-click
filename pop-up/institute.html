Workshops

The main objective of the four workshops (Fitting Shop, Welding Shop, Machine Shop and Electrical shop) is to develop skilled and multi-skilled manpower and produce work order jobs for various departments. Besides new trainees who are trained as Trade Apprentices as per the Apprenticeship Training Scheme, practical training to Technician cum Operative Trainees, regular training courses like Basic Engineering Skills training and other shop based programmes are also conducted for the benefit of semi-skilled as well as skilled workers. The employees are also regularly trade tested to promote them for higher jobs and for this purpose all modern machines and tools have been provided.

Fitting-Shop

There are 70 numbers of Bench Vices, 2 numbers Pillar type Drilling Machines, Bending Machine, Shearing Machine, 2 numbers Pedestal Grinding Machines and 2 numbers Bench Drilling Machines.

Other facilities of Fitting Shop are Material Handling Demonstration Equipment, Eldro- Brake, Hydraulic Press and Rigs for conducting the following Basic Engineering and Multi Skill Training modules:

• Key & Coupling Fitting
• Bearing Fitting
• Overhauling of Gear Box
• Machine Alignment
• Adjustment of Eldro and DC Brakes
• Basic Mechanical Drawing Reading
• Refractory laying Practices
• Material Handling
• Repair & Fitting of Electrical Machines
• Vulcanising of Conveyor Belts
• Wire Rope Splicing

Welding-shop

There are Welding Transformers having 6 regular Generators, MIG/MAG CO2 Welding Machine, TIG Welding Machine with plasma coating, Pedestal Grinding Machine and Gas Cutting and Welding facilities. Following two Multi Skill Training programmes are conducted here:

• Basic Welding & Gas Cutting
• TIG/MIG Welding

Machine-shop

There are 8 numbers of Lathe Machines, CNC Lathe Trainer, 8 numbers of Shaping Machines, Slotting Machine, Radial Drilling Machine, Universal Milling Machine, Horizontal Milling Machine, Boring Machine, 2 numbers of Double Ended Pedestal Grinder, Surface Grinder and Power Saw Metal Cutting Machine . In Machine shop the Apprenticeship Training for Machinist Trade is conducted covering Shaping, Slotting, Planning, Milling, Drilling and Grinding exercises. Turner Trade exercises are conducted on Lathe Machine and Drilling Machine. Sequence of operations relating to the performance of skills are covered in these exercises. In addition to imparting practical training, number of work order jobs received from different departments of RSP are executed here.

Electrical-shop

we have various drives installed for training purpose. There are AC & DC Drives, Transformer Panel, Crane Circuits Panel, Battery Panel, Resistance Boxes and Common Measuring Instruments available. Following 4 Basic Engineering Modules are conducted here:
• Testing / Maintenance / Troubleshooting of AC Motors
• Testing / Maintenance / Troubleshooting of DC Motors
• Study of Electrical and Electronics Drawing
• Electrical Measuring Instruments
• Rewinding of Single Phase AC Motors
